Payment Deadlines

We require a deposit of $750 due at the beginning of each semester. Once the semester begins you have 14 days to make a complete payment on the account. With that being said, our financial aid team at the university is amazing! We work with students where they're at based on their household and income to find a payment plan that works best. If you require this type of assistance, I'd suggest reaching out to our the Financial Aid Department ASAP, as it is a busy time for them.

Please note if you're recieving any government assistance, the Financial Aid Department will be in direct contact with them and notified, there is no need to contact them if your account still shows balance after the 14 days.

If you have any other questions about payment deadlines and tuition fees, please go to the Payment Terms and Deadlines on the university website.

Earth Science Program

The Earth Science program offered by University of Coolness is one of a kind. The university offers courses using the most advanced and innovative technology to study our earths dynamics today. The University of Coolness is one of the only universities of it's kind to offer this program with a focus on hands on learning and field work.

There are many jobs offered with this degree and further educational opportunities to be pursued with this degree. If you'd like to know more about the Earth Science program and what further opportunities this field of study can offer, please visit the Earth Science Degree program page on the university website.

If for whatever reason you feel that Earth Sciences isn't for you, Please let the university know. The university will do their best to work with you in transferring programs and any completed course credits if applicable.

We offer a few programs that are similar to our Earth Science Program.

  1. Study of Geography
  2. Study of Astrology and Astro Dynamics
  3. Study of Geophysics
  4. Study of Oceanography and Biogeography

These programs have courses with some overlap to Earth Sciences and offers similar future paths and levels of study. Should you wish to change the program you originally applied with, please let me know and we may be able to change that for the upcoming Fall semester.
